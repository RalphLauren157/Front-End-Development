About Us

Nestled in the heart of Salford, Manchester, Chiki Ching was born out of a desire to celebrate the vibrant culinary traditions of the city while embracing the diversity of its community. The business was founded in 2024 by childhood friends and Salford locals, Amina and Jack, who share a passion for food, culture, and creating meaningful connections through meals.
